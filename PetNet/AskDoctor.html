Dr. Lara
Thank you for the information. It sounds like Bella might have a respiratory infection, which is fairly common in cats.
I recommend keeping her comfortable and ensuring she stays hydrated.
You can try using a humidifier in the room where she spends most of her time to help with her breathing.
If her symptoms persist for more than a few days or if she stops eating completely, it would be best to bring her in for a check-up.
