Treat Pet Clinic


Location: Irbid,Alrazi st

Working hours : 8 AM - 8 PM



Treat Pet Clinic is a trusted veterinary center committed to offering exceptional medical care for pets. We strive to create a stress-free and comfortable environment where every pet receives the attention and care they deserve.

At Treat Pet Clinic, we believe in building lasting relationships with pet owners by offering personalized care and expert guidance.



Treat Clinic Services

Vaccination

Protect your pet from common diseases with timely vaccinations. Our clinic offers a full range of vaccines to keep your pet healthy.

Grooming

Keep your pet looking and feeling great with professional grooming services. Our groomers provide care that goes beyond aesthetics.

Surgery

Get immediate medical attention for your pet during emergencies. Our clinic is equipped to handle urgent situations and provide critical care.

Dental Care

Keep your pet's teeth and gums healthy with our comprehensive dental care services.


Check-Ups

Ensure your pet's health with comprehensive check-ups. Our veterinarians perform thorough examinations to detect any issues early.
